Sushi

Description

Homemade salmon roll made with sushi rice, nori, and smoked salmon. This is a very basic and easy recipe for making sushi. It might take a while to learn how to make a perfect roll, but you'll have fun trying!

Add wasabi (Japanese horseradish) for a good kick!

Ingredients

  • Sushi rice
  • Rice wine vinegar
  • Seaweed
  • Wasabi paste
  • Smoked salmon
  • Cucumber
  • Avocado

    • Directions

    1. Soak and cook the rice, then mix in the vinegar and cool in an even layer on a plate.
    2. Assemble the sushi rolls based on the detailed recipe below.
    3. Roll the sushi tightly and cut into eight pieces.
